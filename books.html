Reading List

I loved reading as a kid, but as I got older I didn't seem to have time for books anymore. During my senior year of college, I started to pick it back up a little bit and then the pandemic hit and I suddenly had all the time in the world on my hands. Needless to say, I started reading heavily again and I have continued to read consistently (almost every day) since then - it's a habit now. I'm just using this page to keep track of what I read and this will also help to hold me accountable in the future.
